About LesDishes

A chef's touch, made by hand.

LesDishes is a small-batch sourdough bakery and seasonal catering studio founded in 2023, built on the belief that food made slowly tastes better — and feels different on the table.

Every loaf, every spread, every brunch box is made in small batches with intention. From naturally fermented sourdough to thoughtfully curated catering menus, we bring a chef's care to everyday eating.

— Simple elegance in every bite.
